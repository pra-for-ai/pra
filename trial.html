Introduction to the Taxonomy

The Taxonomy of Aspect-Oriented AI Hazards is a fundamental component of the PRA for AI workbook. It acts as a structured framework for systematically identifying and categorizing potential AI hazards during the assessment process. It provides a principles-derived, top-down span of four AI system aspects: AI model capabilities, higher-risk knowledge domains, system features/affordances, and end harms. This taxonomy is further used to derive an extensive list of AI Hazards.

Taxonomy Structure

The Taxonomy of Aspect-Oriented AI Hazards is organized into the following Taxonomy Levels (TLs):

  • TL0: Aspect Category - Broad classes of AI system characteristics
  • TL1: Aspect Group - Major subdivisions within each aspect category
  • TL2: Aspect - Specific topic areas within each aspect group
  • TL3: Subaspect - Prominent thematic areas of relevant aspect-oriented hazards
  • TL4: Aspect-Oriented Hazards - Specific AI hazards related to each subaspect
