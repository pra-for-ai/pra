The PRA for AI Workbook

The PRA for AI workbook is a tool for conducting probabilistic risk assessments of AI systems. It offers an integrated environment for assessing potential risks from AI systems, including all reference objects and protocols for selecting assessment complexity, executing the assessment, and generating a final risk report card.

Workbook Preview

The current version of the workbook is v0.9.0-alpha, representing a working alpha is available for download. This version includes structured support for scanning potential risks through an aspect-oriented taxonomy of AI Hazards, extensive guidance on generating risk scenarios and estimating levels of risk, and detailed instructions on systematically documenting each step of the process. At the conclusion, users receive an auto-generated risk report card summarizing their findings. The report card includes a button that allows users to generate a risk assessment output log - a static version of the entry log that preserves risk scenarios, assumptions, and data for future reference and comparison.

The development team is actively seeking user feedback to enhance and streamline the assessment workflow.
