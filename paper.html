The PRA for AI Paper
OVERVIEW NEED TAXONOMY PAPER WORKBOOK
‎

A forthcoming research paper presents the conceptual foundations, methodology, and rationale behind the PRA framework.

The paper is currently in progress, with a release scheduled along with the workbook tool for Q4 2024. It covers topics such as the landscape of AI safety methods, the adaptation of PRA to AI risks, sources that inform a hazards taxonomy, and a presentation of the framework. Future papers are planned to be released based on the framework as well, with plans to include case studies and further assessment guidance.

Watch this space to get the paper when it's published, or contact us to be notified when it does become available.
