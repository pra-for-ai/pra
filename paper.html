Adapting Probabilistic Risk Assessment for AI

This page provides access to the research paper detailing the conceptual foundations, methodology, rationale, and context for the PRA for AI framework.

Citation:

Wisakanto, A. K., Rogero, J., Casheekar, A. M., & Mallah, R. (2025). Adapting Probabilistic Risk Assessment for AI. arXiv preprint arXiv:2504.18536. [cs.AI] 25 Apr 2025.

The paper covers:

  • The landscape of AI risk and limitations of current assessment methods.
  • The adaptation of traditional PRA techniques for AI systems.
  • Detailed explanations of Aspect-Oriented Hazard Analysis, Risk Pathway Modeling, and Uncertainty Management.
  • The structure and intended use of the framework components.
  • Discussion of applications, limitations, and future research directions.

Preprint released April 2025.
